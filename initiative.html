INITIATIVE
  • Enter unique names for all entities in initiative.
  • Set their initiative bonus dexterity attributes
  • Use tab to quickly switch between edit fields
  • Click 'Generate' to randomize initiative create an order
  • Hit 'Sort' to restablish initiative order
  • Hit 'Start' or spacebar to begin counting initiative
  • Use 'Next' or spacebar to iterate
  • Use and to change order
  • Use to drop an entry to the end of the queue (hold their turn)
  • Use to remove someone from initiative and to bring them back in (at the end of the queue)
  • 'Clear' will delete all entries
  • Hit 'Stop' or esc to exit initiative. Sorting, generating, and clearing will also end Initiative
  • Don't worry about having to re-enter characrters and stats for each session, the tracker will hold it's state as long as you don't clear your browser data!
     
  • "a" = Highlight Add Character
  • "g" = Generate
  • "s" = Sort
  • "space" = Start/advance initiative
  • "esc" = End initiative/deselect field
  • "enter" = Add character (when selected)
