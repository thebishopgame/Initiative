INITIATIVE
Name:
   
  • Enter names for all entities in initiative.
  • Set their initiative and dexterity scores
  • Hit 'Sort'
  • Hit 'Start' or spacebar to begin counting initiative
  • Use 'Next' or spacebar to iterate
  • Use 'Up' and 'Down' to change order
  • Use 'Hold' to drop someone to the end of the queue (hold their turn)
  • Hit 'Stop' or esc to exit initiative. Sorting will also end Initiative
