INITIATIVE
  • Enter names for all entities in initiative.
  • Set their initiative and dexterity scores
  • Use tab to quickly switch between character initiatives
  • Hit 'Sort' to establish initiative order
  • Hit 'Start' or spacebar to begin counting initiative
  • Use 'Next' or spacebar to iterate
  • Use and to change order
  • Use to drop an entry to the end of the queue (hold their turn)
  • Use to remove someone from initiative and to bring them back in (at the end of the queue)
  • Hit 'Stop' or esc to exit initiative. Sorting will also end Initiative
  • 'Reset Init' will set all initiatives back to 10
  • 'Clear' will delete all entries
